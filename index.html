What is P2P trading?

P2P means peer-to-peer. Instead of buying or selling crypto through a traditional exchange order book, you trade directly with another person using an agreed payment method and price.

How it works

  • You choose an offer or create one.
  • You send or receive payment using an approved payment method.
  • The platform holds the crypto in escrow until the trade is completed.
  • Once payment is confirmed, the crypto is released.

Why people use it

  • Flexible payment methods like bank transfer or Wise.
  • Better control over pricing and trade limits.
  • Useful for people who prefer direct trading with clear terms.
  • A practical way to learn crypto usage beyond just holding coins.

Important safety rules

These are the habits that help you avoid common problems in P2P trading.

Do this

  • Use your own verified payment account.
  • Read the offer terms before opening a trade.
  • Stay calm and communicate clearly.
  • Double-check names and payment details.
  • Start with low amounts while learning.

Avoid this

  • Releasing crypto before confirming payment.
  • Accepting third-party payments.
  • Rushing because someone pressures you.
  • Ignoring the platform’s safety process.
  • Trading amounts you cannot afford to risk.
