Lesson 1: What is P2P Trading?

P2P means peer-to-peer. It is a way of buying and selling cryptocurrency directly with another person using an agreed payment method and price.

How it works

  • You choose an offer or create one.
  • You send or receive payment using an approved payment method.
  • The platform holds the crypto in escrow during the trade.
  • After payment is confirmed, the crypto is released.

Simple example

A seller has USDT. You send payment through your bank. Once the seller confirms payment, the crypto is released to your wallet.

Lesson 2: Why People Use P2P Trading

Many users like P2P because it gives them more flexibility, more payment options, and more direct control over the trade.

Main benefits

  • Flexible payment methods like bank transfer.
  • Better control over price and trade limits.
  • Direct trading with other users.
  • A practical entry point for learning crypto usage.

Beginner note

P2P can be simple when you follow the process carefully. The biggest mistakes usually happen when beginners rush or ignore safety rules.

Lesson 4: Important Safety Rules

Good safety habits can help you avoid common beginner mistakes and reduce unnecessary risk.

Do this

  • Use your own verified payment account.
  • Read trade terms before opening a transaction.
  • Double-check payment details.
  • Stay calm and communicate clearly.
  • Start with smaller amounts while learning.

Avoid this

  • Releasing crypto before confirming payment.
  • Accepting third-party payments.
  • Rushing because someone pressures you.
  • Ignoring the platform’s safety process.
  • Trading amounts you cannot afford to risk.
