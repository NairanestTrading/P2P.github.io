📘 Lesson 1: What is P2P Trading?

P2P means peer-to-peer. Instead of buying or selling crypto from a traditional exchange order book, you trade directly with another person using an agreed price and payment method.

How it works

  • You choose an offer or create one.
  • You send or receive payment using an approved payment method.
  • The platform holds the crypto in escrow during the trade.
  • Once payment is confirmed, the crypto is released.

Simple example

A seller has USDT. You pay them through your bank or approved payment method. After the seller confirms the money, the crypto is released to your wallet.

📘 Lesson 2: Why People Use P2P Trading

Many people use P2P because it gives more flexibility, more payment options, and a practical way to buy or sell crypto directly.

Main benefits

  • Flexible payment methods such as bank transfer.
  • Better control over price and trade limits.
  • Direct interaction with other traders.
  • Useful for people who want simple buying and selling options.

Beginner note

P2P can be simple, but only if you stay patient, follow the platform rules, and avoid rushing into trades before you understand the process.

📘 Lesson 4: Important Safety Rules

Safety is one of the most important parts of P2P trading. These habits help reduce avoidable mistakes.

Do this

  • Use your own verified payment account.
  • Read offer terms before opening a trade.
  • Stay calm and communicate clearly.
  • Double-check payment details.
  • Start with low amounts while learning.

Avoid this

  • Releasing crypto before confirming payment.
  • Accepting third-party payments.
  • Rushing because someone pressures you.
  • Ignoring platform safety steps.
  • Trading amounts you cannot afford to risk.
